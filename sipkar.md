# Privacy Policy for SIPKar

**Effective Date:** 19 January 2026  
**Developer:** Laugh Logic Labs  
**Contact Email:** info@laughlogiclabs.com  

**SIPKar is an educational and financial planning application.**

---

## 1. Purpose of the Application

SIPKar is built **only for educational, learning, and financial planning purposes**.

- The App provides calculators such as **SIP, Lumpsum, SWP, and Delayed SIP**.
- The App **does not provide investment advice**, financial recommendations, stock tips, or guidance to buy, sell, or hold any financial product.
- All results are **illustrative estimates** based purely on user inputs and standard mathematical formulas.
- SIPKar is **not a financial advisor**, broker, or investment platform.

Users are advised to consult a certified financial professional before making any investment decisions.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**SIPKar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or user information.**

This includes, but is not limited to:
- Name, email address, or phone number  
- Financial information (bank details, PAN, Aadhaar, account numbers)  
- Location data  
- Device identifiers or analytics  
- Calculator inputs or results  

All calculations are performed **locally on the user’s device**.

---

## 3. Advertisements

SIPKar displays advertisements to support ongoing development and future feature improvements.

- Advertisements are served by third-party ad providers such as **Google AdMob**.
- SIPKar itself does **not collect or access** any data collected by ad providers.
- Ad providers may collect limited non-personal data (such as Advertising ID, IP address, or ad interaction data) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

More information about Google’s advertising practices can be found at:  
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## 4. Local Processing Only

- Users enter calculator values and tap the **Calculate** button.
- Results are generated instantly on the device.
- No data is saved, uploaded, or synced to any server.
- Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored app data automatically.
